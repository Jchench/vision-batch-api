SEC. 812. POST-RELEASE SUPERVISION OF TERRORISTS.

Section 3583 of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
“(j) SUPERVISED RELEASE TERMS FOR TERRORIST PREDICATES.—
Notwithstanding subsection (b), the authorized term of supervised release for any offense listed in section 2332b(g)(5)(B), the commis- sion of which resulted in, or created a foreseeable risk of, death or serious bodily injury to another person, is any term of years or life.”.
