		         	        EFFECT ON OTHER LAWS

SEC. 609. Section 111(a) of the Truth in Lending Act (15 U.S.C. 1610(a)) is amended to read as follows:
	'' (a)(1) Chapters 1, 2, and 3 do not annul, alter, or affect the laws of any State relating to the disclosure of information in connection with credit transactions, except to the extent that those laws are inconsistent with the provisions of this title, and then only to the extent of the inconsistency. Upon its own motion or upon the request of any creditor, State, or other interested party which is submitted in accordance with procedures prescribed in regulations of the Board, the Board shall determine whether any such inconsistency exists.
