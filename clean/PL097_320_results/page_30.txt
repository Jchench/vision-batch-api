	" (iv) in the event of dissolution of a mutual association after conversion, the members or shareholders of the association will share in a mutual basis in the assets of the association in exact proportion to their relative share or account credits;

	"(v) in the event of dissolution of a stock association after conversion, the stockholders will share on a equitable basis in the assets of the association; and

	" (vi) such conversion shall be effective upon the date that all the provisions of this Act have been fully complied with and upon the issuance of a new charter by the State wherein the association is located.
